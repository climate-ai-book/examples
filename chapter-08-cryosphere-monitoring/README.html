Key Concepts and Mathematical Foundations

1. Ice Energy Balance

Fundamental Energy Equation:

ρ_i c_i ∂T/∂t = k_i ∇²T + Q_rad + Q_sens + Q_lat + Q_cond
Where:

ρ_i: ice density
c_i: specific heat capacity
T: temperature
k_i: thermal conductivity
Q terms: radiative, sensible, latent, and conductive heat fluxes

2. Ice Velocity Neural Network

Velocity Prediction:

v_ice = f_neural(τ, T, ρ, h) + v_basal(φ, p_w)
Inputs:

τ: stress
T: temperature
ρ: density
h: thickness
φ: bed slope
p_w: water pressure

3. Permafrost Temperature

Subsurface Heat Transfer:

∂T_soil/∂t = α_soil ∂²T_soil/∂z² + (1/ρ_soil c_soil)[Q_surface + Q_geothermal]
Active Layer Thickness:
ALT = ∫₀^z_thaw H(T_soil(z,t_max) - T_freeze) dz

4. Sea Ice Classification

CNN for Ice Type Detection:

P_ice(x,y) = σ(Σ_k W_k * I_SAR(x,y) + b_k)
Ice types: new ice, young ice, first-year ice, multi-year ice

Multi-Sensor Fusion:

F_fusion = Attention(F_SAR, F_optical) · W_temporal + F_contextual

5. Glacier Boundary Detection

Edge Detection:

E_glacier(x,y) = max_{s∈S} |∇[G_s * I_satellite(x,y)]| · M_learned(x,y)
Surface Velocity:
v_surface(x,y,t) = (1/Δt)[r(x,y,t+Δt) - r(x,y,t)] + v_correction

6. Permafrost Neural Prediction

LSTM Model:

T_permafrost(z,t) = f_neural(T_air(t), P_precip(t), R_solar(t), W_wind(t), θ_soil, V_veg)
Environmental inputs:

Air temperature
Precipitation
Solar radiation
Wind speed
Soil properties
Vegetation cover

7. Radio-Echo Sounding

Signal Processing:

S_processed(t) = CNN(W_filter * S_raw(t) + b) · G_gain(t)
Ice Thickness:
h_ice = (c·t_travel)/(2√ε_r) · Correction_neural(θ, ρ_ice, T)

8. Ensemble Methods

Ensemble Prediction:

ŷ_ensemble = Σ_i w_i·ŷ_i + ε_ensemble
Bayesian Uncertainty:
p(y*|x*,D) = ∫ p(y*|x*,θ) p(θ|D) dθ

9. Validation Metrics

Comprehensive Assessment:

V_cryo = α·RMSE_spatial + β·Corr_temporal + γ·Bias_systematic + δ·Skill_physical
